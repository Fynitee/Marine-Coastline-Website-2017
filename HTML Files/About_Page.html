about

  • The Marine Coastline is an aquarium tourist attraction that promotes and encourages the preservation of marine life.
  • The Marine Coastline project first began in 1997 and was started by Isabella Cheng.
  • In this aquarium, 20% of the profits will go towards organizations dedicated to preserving
    marine life such as the Nautical Preservation Society and the Marinal Reef Association.
  • The Marine Coastline is proud to receive three certifications in 2017 as reward for the
    hard work that the staff members of the Marine Coastline have put in.
